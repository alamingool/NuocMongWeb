About Nuoc Mong

Become the Puppeter

In Nuoc Mong, you aren't just watching a show; you are the unseen hand guiding the performance. Set against the backdrop of a traditional Vietnamese water stage, you will interact with the scene using your hands, detected via a standard webcam. This innovative control method allows for a fluid and intuitive connection with the puppets and their world, blurring the lines between player and performer.

Unfold Ancient Tales

The game presents a series of captivating vignettes, each telling a story from Vietnamese folklore – from playful tales of villagers and animals to heroic legends and spiritual journeys. Your interaction isn't about solving puzzles in a traditional sense, but about interpreting and influencing the performance through your control over the puppets and stage elements. It's about expressing the narrative and feeling the rhythm of the show.

A Living Painting

Every element of Nuoc Mong, from the handcrafted look of the puppets to the shimmering surface of the water and the atmospheric stage lighting, is designed to evoke the magic of a live water puppetry performance. The visuals are complemented by a rich soundscape featuring traditional instruments, creating an experience that is both visually stunning and culturally resonant.
